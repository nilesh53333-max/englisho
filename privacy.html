Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2025

Speaku ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our English speaking practice service via WhatsApp and our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Speaku, we may collect the following information:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your name (if provided)
  • Conversation messages you send to our chatbot
  • Your mother tongue and target language preference
  • Usage data such as number of sessions and messages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our language learning service
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Process payments for premium subscriptions
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our product

3. AI Conversations

Your conversations with Speaku are processed by AI language models (Google Gemini) to generate responses. Conversations may be stored temporarily to maintain context within a session. We do not sell your conversation data to third parties.

4. WhatsApp Data

Speaku operates through the WhatsApp Business API. By messaging Speaku on WhatsApp, you agree to WhatsApp's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We only access messages sent directly to our business number.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Service providers who help us operate our platform (AiSensy, Google)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of our service at any time by messaging "STOP" to our WhatsApp number

9. Children's Privacy

Speaku is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
